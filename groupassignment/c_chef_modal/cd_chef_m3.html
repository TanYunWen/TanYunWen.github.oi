Zhou Fei

Mr. Li Guoxiong has more than 30 years of working experience in the baking industry. He is also the first industry celebrity in the baking industry to receive the National Technical Expert Award and the Chinese Famous Chef Five-Star Medal. As a generation of cake decoration art masters, he has been devoted to the research of cake decoration art for many years. He integrates the essence of Chinese and Western cultures, constantly innovates, has the courage to open up, and studies hard, making the "Li Guoxiong" cake a well-known Chinese baking industry.

1. 1987 - Published his first professional cake album, creating a precedent for customers to order cakes by looking at pictures in a cake shop.

2. 1993 - Made the first plastic simulation cake in China.

3. 2001 & 2005 - The first ceramic cake was produced, developed and manufactured the artificial soft plastic sculpture, and both obtained a national patent.

4. 2007 - on the basis of the sugar flower cake, Mr. Li Guoxiong first invented the clay-kneading simulation cake, which subconsciously attracted fashionable young people and children.

5. 2008 - Creatively extends teaching into a more personalized, humane world of cakes. In the creative DIY cake world, professional talents, professional equipment, and excellence integrate famous brand products from all over the world, Western culture, and lifestyle to lead the trend, give full play to the temptation of cakes, and make cake culture more widely The spread of the cake makes the cake world appear more perfect.

6. 2014 - Became one of the drafters of "GB/T 31059-2014 National Standard of the People's Republic of China - Decorated Cake".
