Zhou Fei

2014 - Zhou graduated from a vocational school in Hangzhou, Zhejiang province, majoring in Western desserts and buttercream cakes, to become a pastry chef.

He found that fondant cakes have more room for creative customization for clients, so he started to learn fondant modeling by himself. Moreover, Zhou wanted "to use this Western skill to present traditional Chinese culture" and he has actually made a name out of it.

"A fondant cake with exquisite details can impress the clients as if silently telling a story," he says.

After years of effort, and armed with his wild imagination, he has become well-known in the field for creating fondant figures from Chinese myth.

2018 - "magic forest" which won him a top prize at the International Cake Show Australia.
